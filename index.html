Search Help

Enter any text to filter all cards based on their names, tags, and descriptions.

Searches are not case sensitive, and you can enclose text in "quotes" to search for more complex phrases. Cards will only appear if they have all the terms you enter.

To search for values for a particular property, enter the property name, followed by a colon, folowed by the value to search for: initiative:4.

Supported properties include: type, initiative, name, cooldown, variant (basic, alpha, or beta), effects (ongoing or interrupt), spends (surge, doublesurge, star, or numbers), abundance (number in the game), price, and currency (gold or soulshards).

To search for game symbols in text, name them in parenthesis: (Standard Die), (Enhancement Die), (Cursed Die), (Power Die), (Blessing Die), (Plus), (PlusPlus), or (Star).
